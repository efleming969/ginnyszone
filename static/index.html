About

Ginny has been a licensed hair stylist for 15 years and has been practicing in the Wesley Chapel and the surrounding area since 2003. She trained exclusively with the Redken Color Line and specializes in color corrections and highlights.

She offers many services including haircuts, texturizing, shampoos, blow dry’s, formal styling, and conditioning treatments. Whether you need a cut and color for a special event or for day to day maintenance, Ginny can and will make you look your best.

Appointments can be booked by calling the salon at 813-695-6219 or you can visit her Facebook page at facebook.com/ginnyszone.
